Who is a sibling care partner?

We define a "sibling care partner" as a sibling living with a developmental disability and/or a sibling who cares for a sibling with a developmental disability. The partnership is such that both persons involved are in a committed lifelong relationship to care for each other in different ways with compassion, compromise and collaboration. "Care" can look different in different care partnerships, but the baseline is that you care about the partner (your sibling in this case).
